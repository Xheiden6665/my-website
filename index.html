Our Story

Building excellence one tile at a time

Founded in 2015, ALPHA REMODELING has grown from a small local business to a trusted name in premium tile and stone installation. With over 14 years of combined experience, our team brings unparalleled expertise to every project.

We specialize in custom tile work for residential and commercial spaces, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. From elegant bathroom renovations to expansive commercial installations, we approach each project with the same commitment to quality and attention to detail.

What sets us apart is our dedication to using only the finest materials and our ability to bring our clients' visions to life with precision and artistry.
